About Us

Welcome to our company Business is Boomin! As students at The Howard University (The Number one HBCU) we see a constant need for leaders. Being in the nation's capital inspires us to push the bounds of what we can do every day. In our journey to inspire, we've seen a lot of problems, especially in young entrepreneurs. Wisdom comes with experience, and a lot of us don't have that. Our app aims to bridge that gap. Many people nowadays have ideas or things they want to do yet they don't have the knowledge on where to start, or what needs to be achieved before they can take the next step. Business Boom AI helps users quickly pass much of the preliminary struggles of planning a business, and keeps the most important things (SWOT, Costs, Break-Even) at the forefront. In a world where AI is growing, entrepreneurship is becoming more valuable than ever. And we're here to help!
